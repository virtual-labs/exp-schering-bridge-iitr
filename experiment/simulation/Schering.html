STEP 1 : Make Connections as per the instructions given below:

1.    M1 of MCB - M3 of Kit

2.    M2 of MCB - M4 of Kit

3.    D1 of Digital Null Detector - D3 of Circuit Diagram

4.    D2 of Digital Null Detector - D4 of Circuit Diagram

5.    X1 - S1 of Capacitor

6.    X2 - S2 of Capacitor

NOTE : If wire is wrongly connected, Click on node number to detach the wire.

STEP 2 : Click on "CHECK" button for checking the connections.

STEP 3 : Click on the MCB to Turn ON the supply.

STEP 4 : Vary x10Ω knob by selecting the values from dropdown until you reach the minimum value of the digital null detector.

STEP 5 : Now, Vary x100Ω knob by selecting the values from dropdown until you get the minimum value of the digital null detector.

STEP 6 : Click on "ADD" button to add the readings to the Observation Table.

NOTE : If combination is wrong then select another combination & the balanced condition button becomes active after the wrong combination alert.

STEP 7 : Click on "PRINT" button to print the webpage.

STEP 8 : Click on "RESET" button to reset the webpage.

FORMULA

    Find C1 (Unknown Capacitance)

    C1 = R1/R2 * C2

    where R1 = x10Ω + x100Ω
